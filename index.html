ABOUT US

At FOODoko, we are anchored by a commitment to excellent, responsibly-sourced seafood and warm southern hospitality, serving superior ingredients, exceptional in quality with a homestyle and welcoming attitude.

Our downhome flavors are inspired by an Asian culinary aesthetic and infused with modern taste and a Florida sensibility.

Foodoko serves up made to order and customizable sushi burritos, sushi poke bowls, and sushi rolls to cater to guests' own unique tastes, or select from one of our own master chef's sushi recommendations. Catering options are also available.

Sushi burritos are hand held, easy to eat, and can be quite healthy. There's a reason why the Japanese have some of the longest living people on the planet – this trifecta of fresh fish, rice, and seaweed are very healthy and nutritious.

FOODoko is a play on the Japanese word "fudokoto" meaning "food court". The founders hope to bring the accessibility and convenience of delightful cuisine found at food courts from around Japan and the Pacific to our customers. FOODoko is founded with a vision of bringing the rich cultural traditions and experiences of Japanese and Asian cuisine to our customers.

We are a brand new sushi fast-paced restaurant that is opening in November 8th, 2016 in the Lake Nona area of Orlando, FL and Lake Nona VA Medical Center. We are currently looking for new team members to join us on this new adventure!
