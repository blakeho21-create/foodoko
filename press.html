A few of the places we've been featured

Orlando Weekly•June 27, 2017

Orlando Sentinel Highlights FOODoko Poke Bowls

In a summer feature on poke — cubed raw fish marinated and paired with everything from edamame to mango — the Orlando Sentinel spotlighted FOODoko's grab-and-go bowls, calling out the Sunshine Poke Bowl with tuna, salmon, avocado, cucumber, masago and more.

Source: Orlando Sentinel

Orlando Sentinel•April 6, 2017

FOODoko Mentioned in Orlando Sentinel

A round-up on the rise of the sushi burrito featured FOODoko, where the handheld rolls come wrapped in seaweed and packed with protein, vegetables and sauces.

Source: Orlando Sentinel

Orlando Sentinel•November 10, 2016

Orlando Sentinel Foodie Video Highlights FOODoko

The Sentinel's foodie video introduced FOODoko's sushi burritos to Orlando's Lake Nona, noting the partnership of Yum Yum Asia Cafe founder Steve Kuo and chef Wei Hao Chen and the growing popularity of creative sushi bowls and burritos.

Source: Orlando Sentinel

Orlando Weekly•November 8, 2016

FOODoko Opening Covered in Orlando Weekly

Orlando Weekly's Tip Jar covered opening day, introducing the new quick-stop concept from chefs Steve Kuo (Yum Yum Asia Cafe) and Wei Hao Chen — open to the public inside the Lake Nona VA Medical Center, weekdays 10:30 a.m. to 4 p.m.

Source: Orlando Weekly
